I tested AstroWarp, Tailscale, ZeroTier, and WireGuard together on a GL.iNet Flint 3 running Mesh Alpha firmware.

The interesting part: the services can coexist technically.

The issue is that GL.iNet's management layer may disable Tailscale and ZeroTier when AstroWarp is enabled by flipping their UCI settings.

I documented the full troubleshooting flow, the commands, and the recovery script.
